Zero-Tolerance Environmental Control: Killing the Attraction

Technical Deep Dive: The Right Way to Use an Ultrasonic Deterrent

The market is saturated with cheap ultrasonic pest control devices that do absolutely nothing but offer false hope. If you want to use a high-frequency deterrent, you need to understand the science, or you’re wasting your money.

  3. Power Management: Ensure your deterrent is running on its own dedicated low-voltage battery or a solar trickle charger. Plugging a high-draw unit directly into your car battery is just asking for a dead battery and a new set of problems.

The best defense is an integrated system: use exclusion to make the space inaccessible, and use a properly configured ultrasonic unit to make the area a sensory nightmare, actively discouraging inspection by newcomers.
