Live Weight vs. Hanging Weight vs. Finished Weight: The Meat Math That Saves You Money

Now, for the kicker: The meat you actually take homeâ€”the Finished Weightâ€”is only a fraction of that Hanging Weight. You lose bone, excess fat, and trim. Concrete Example: The True Cost

  1. Live Cow: 1,200 lbs (The animal walking around).
  2. Hanging Weight (HW): $\approx 720 \text{ lbs}$ (60% of live weight). This is what you pay the farmer for.
  3. Finished Weight (Take-Home): $\approx 432 \text{ lbs}$ (60% of HW). This is the actual meat.
