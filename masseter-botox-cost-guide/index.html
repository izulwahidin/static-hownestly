  • The Exception: A portion of the cost might be covered only if your procedure is classified as therapeutic treatment. This requires:

    • Proof that you have exhausted other conventional treatments (like nightguards or physical therapy) without success.
    • Your procedure must be done by a licensed medical provider who can properly bill the procedure with the correct CPT codes (and often a Letter of Medical Necessity).

The Action Step: Do not simply assume coverage. Consult your specific medical insurance provider first.
