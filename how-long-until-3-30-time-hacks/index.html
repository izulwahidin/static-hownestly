‚è±Ô∏è How to Figure Out “How Long Until”

Here’s the deal: figuring out how much time is left until 3:30 PM is super easy. You just need to break the problem into two quick steps.

You’re basically doing a little road trip on the clock. You’re finding the distance between your current time and 3:30.

Step 1: Get to the Next Full Hour

First, look at your clock right now. Let’s pretend it’s 9:14 AM (my time right now).

You want to know how many minutes are left until the clock hits 10:00 AM.

  • There are 60 minutes in an hour, right?
  • You’re already at 14 minutes past the hour.
  • So, you do $60 – 14$.
  • That gives you 46 minutes until 10:00 AM. That’s your first chunk of time.

Step 2: Count the Hours Left

Okay, so you’ve made it to the next full hour (10:00 AM in my example). Now, how many full hours are there between 10:00 AM and 3:00 PM?

This part is simple counting!

  • 10:00 AM to 11:00 AM (1 hour)
  • 11:00 AM to 12:00 PM (2 hours)
  • 12:00 PM to 1:00 PM (3 hours)
  • 1:00 PM to 2:00 PM (4 hours)
  • 2:00 PM to 3:00 PM (5 hours)

That’s 5 full hours until 3:00 PM.

Step 3: Add Up the Remaining Minutes

Now we’re at 3:00 PM. But you wanted to get to 3:30 PM.

  • That’s an extra 30 minutes.

So, you just add up all your little time chunks:

  • 46 minutes (from Step 1)
    • 5 hours (from Step 2)
    • 30 minutes (from Step 3)

$$46 \text{ minutes} + 30 \text{ minutes} = 76 \text{ minutes}$$

And because 76 minutes is more than an hour, you take away 60 minutes and turn that into 1 hour.

  • 76 minutes $\rightarrow$ 1 hour and 16 minutes.

So, if it’s 9:14 AM, you add the times:

  • 5 hours
    • 1 hour and 16 minutes
