How to Bleed the Tank: The Essential 10-Second Pressure Reset

This is the most critical step in the entire process of how to fill a butane lighter, so pay attention. You are not just letting out the last bit of flame; you are emptying the pressure. If you don’t hear a strong hiss, you haven’t done it right.

  1. Locate the Valve: Find the refill valve on the bottom of the lighter (it looks like a metal circle with a tiny opening).
  2. Invert the Lighter: Hold the lighter upside down. This is crucial because it positions the valve at the top of the gas tank, allowing the residual air and low-pressure vapor to escape.
