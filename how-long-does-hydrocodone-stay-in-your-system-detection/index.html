For hydrocodone, that half-life is usually around 4 to 8 hours.

So, if you take 10mg, your body takes about 4 to 8 hours to get down to 5mg. Then another 4 to 8 hours to get to 2.5mg. And so on. It takes about five half-lives for the drug to be almost completely gone from your system.

Bottom line: In your blood, the main effects and most of the drug are gone within one day of your last pill.

Blood tests are usually used right after an accident or if a doctor needs to know exactly how much drug is in your body right now.
