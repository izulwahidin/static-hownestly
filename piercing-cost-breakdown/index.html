If you are wondering why some places charge \$20 and others charge \$80, look no further than the tool used. This is where expertise (E) and technical authority (A) are built into the price.

Tool Upfront Cost (Service + Jewelry) Method Tissue Damage Sterilization Risk Level

The piercing gun is the ultimate culprit behind the misleadingly cheap price point.
