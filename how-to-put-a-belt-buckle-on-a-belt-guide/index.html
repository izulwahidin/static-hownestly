The Universal Rule: Match the Buckle Width to the Belt Width (No Exceptions)

You’d think this is obvious, but most DIY buckle guides skip the single most critical step that prevents 90% of failures. Before you even look at how your belt is attached, you must confirm the dimensional compatibility.

To get this right, you need two measurements:

The two measurements must match precisely. If the buckle opening is too narrow, the belt won’t pass through the chape (the end section of the belt that wraps around the buckle).
