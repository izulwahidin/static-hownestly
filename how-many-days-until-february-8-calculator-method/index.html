The Simple Formula: Current Day Number + Subtraction

Now that we know February 8 is Day 39, the rest is just simple subtraction. Think of it like a countdown. You just need to figure out what “day number” today is.

Here‚Äôs the Foolproof Formula:

$$\text{Days Remaining} = \text{Target Day Number} – \text{Current Day Number}$$

Since our target is February 8, the formula is:

$$\text{Days Remaining} = 39 – \text{Today’s Day Number}$$

Step 2: Add the Next Year’s Days (Up to February 8)
