The troy ounce (ozt) is the official, mandated unit of measurement for all precious metals trading worldwide. It has been since the Middle Ages, when it was used in Troyes, France. The precise weight of the troy ounce is:

$$1 \text{ troy ounce} = 31.1034768 \text{ grams}$$

The Conversion Error That Costs You Money (Troy vs. Avoirdupois)
