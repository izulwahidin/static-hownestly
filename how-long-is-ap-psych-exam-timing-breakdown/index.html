You are not writing an English essay here. You don’t need fancy introductions or conclusions. The people grading your test (the graders are called readers, by the way) are looking for one thing: Did you correctly define the term and apply it to the scenario?

Use the terms as headings or bold them in your answer. It makes the reader’s job easier, and an easy-to-read answer is way more likely to get the points.

Seriously, use the terms as subheadings. Don’t make the grader hunt for your answer. If the term is “Functional Fixedness,” write: Functional Fixedness: [Your definition]… [Your application to the scenario].
