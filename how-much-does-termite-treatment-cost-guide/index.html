Liquid Barrier Treatment (The Perimeter Defense)

The key to a professional job lies in the chemical choice. If your contractor is still using a repellent termiticide, fire them. Repellents like Bifenthrin create a “no-go” zone that termites detect and, crucially, will find a tiny, microscopic gap to breach. The professional standard, what you actually want, is a non-repellent liquid treatment like Termidor (Fipronil). This product is undetectable; termites walk right through it, unknowingly picking up the poison and carrying it back to the colony via contact (a process called trophallaxis).
