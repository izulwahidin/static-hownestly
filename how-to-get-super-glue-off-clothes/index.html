  • Remove: Take the item out and immediately start cracking the hardened glue. Use a blunt object, like the back of a spoon handle, or simply use your fingers to flex the fabric and break the brittle glue mass. The goal is to chip it off in pieces.
  • Cleanup: Immediately vacuum up the shards to prevent them from catching on the fabric again.
