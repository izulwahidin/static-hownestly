This is a pro move. Take a piece of cardboard or an old towel and place it inside the garment, right beneath the oil stain.

If dish soap isn’t cutting it, you need to go harder. Lots of people swear by WD-40 or a laundry pre-treatment spray that specifically mentions “grease” or “oil.”

  • Add a bit of a stain fighter booster, if you have one. Borax or OxiClean are awesome choices.
